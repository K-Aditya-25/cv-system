# CommunityForge - Startup Events & Technology Community Coordinator

CommunityForge supports technology communities, early-stage founders, and student builders through events, workshops, and founder programmes.

Responsibilities include:

- Plan and deliver startup, innovation, and technology community events.
- Coordinate speakers, mentors, sponsors, volunteers, venues, and attendees.
- Communicate clearly with founders, students, partners, and internal stakeholders.
- Support outreach campaigns, sponsorship conversations, and community partnerships.
- Create content such as event recaps, newsletters, founder interviews, and speaker notes.
- Use lightweight tools to manage event operations, registration, and follow-up.

Useful experience:

- Startup ecosystem involvement, event operations, and community building.
- Strong written and verbal communication.
- Stakeholder management, sponsorship outreach, and public speaking.
- Comfort with technology teams, builders, and technical project work.
